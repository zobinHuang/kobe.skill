Legendary basketball player Kobe Bryant joined an impressive roster of A-list celebrities at Alibaba's 11.11 Countdown Gala Celebration in Shenzhen, held on November 10, 2016, 24 hours before the company's major shopping festival.

The four-hour live event featured interactive elements designed to engage customers, including mobile engagement through the Taobao and Tmall apps, interactive voting, and user-generated content segments.

Other participants included OneRepublic, Katy Perry (global ambassador for the event), Beth Behrs, and Thomas Muller.

Alibaba Group CMO Chris Tung described the gala as "a global carnival, a world-class performance that involves the audience throughout the event with many touch points."
