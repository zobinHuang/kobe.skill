# ESPN The Magazine Inaugural Issue - "NEXT." (March 1998)

The first issue of ESPN The Magazine, with the cover line "NEXT.", was published on March 11, 1998 (cover date March 23, 1998), and featured Kobe Bryant of the NBA alongside Alex Rodriguez (MLB), Kordell Stewart (NFL), and Eric Lindros (NHL).

The inaugural issue positioned Bryant as the face of the "next" generation of professional sports, cementing his status as the post-Jordan era's most promising young athlete. The original article text is not available online.
