I don't think people understand how important writing is. You write to figure things out about yourself, and then try to interpret it. As basketball players, we're really supposed to shut up and dribble? I mean, I'm glad we do a little bit more than that. Thank you, Academy, for this incredible honor. Thank you, John Williams, for such a wonderful piece of music. Thank you, Verizon, for believing in the project from the start. To my wife Vanessa, our daughters Natalia, Gianna, and Bianka... you are my inspiration. I mean that from the depths of my heart. Thank you so much. And to all of our young people out there: if you want to be great at something there's a choice you have to make. What I mean by that is, there are negotiables and there are non-negotiables. And you have to be honest with yourself about what you are willing to sacrifice. You have to know that the dream that you have for yourself is worth that sacrifice. Thank you.
