# Kobe Bryant Through the Years: SI Coverage

This content is from the SI retrospective compilation "Kobe Bryant Through the Years" which references this cover story period. The original individual article is no longer available as a standalone piece online.

## Key Themes from SI's Coverage

### Championship Era (2000-2010)
Bryant won five titles, including early championships alongside Shaquille O'Neal. Observers noted his fearlessness and refusal to be constrained by circumstance. After O'Neal's departure, Bryant captured his fourth title in 2009, proving he could win independently.

### The Great Unknown (2006)
Following the Shaq split and legal troubles, SI explored Bryant's intense compartmentalization and isolated psyche. He channeled all energy into basketball, becoming the most feared scorer in the game.

### Satisfaction (2009)
Winning without Shaq validated Bryant's approach. He could lead a team his way to a championship.

### Kobe's Final Challenge (2010)
Back-to-back championships cemented his legacy alongside the all-time greats.
