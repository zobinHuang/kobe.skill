ESPN's Special Kobe Bryant Magazine Issue

ESPN released a special 96-page commemorative magazine titled "Kobe Bryant: An Extraordinary Life" on Friday, February 3, 2020. The issue does not carry ESPN the Magazine branding.

The publication features photographs from Bryant's career and written contributions from various ESPN personalities who covered the Los Angeles Lakers legend, including host Stephen A. Smith.

Alison Overholt, ESPN's vice president of storytelling and special projects, explained the approach: "We specifically limited this to be a test case to do the work, see how the process goes and see if this makes sense. We'll evaluate when and how often it makes sense to do something like this."

This special edition represents ESPN's plan to release "a handful" of commemorative issues annually following the discontinuation of ESPN the Magazine's print edition in September 2019 after 21 years.

The issue honors Bryant and his daughter Gianna, who died alongside seven others in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California in January 2020.
